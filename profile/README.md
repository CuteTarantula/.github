# About Us

[Cute Tarantula Ltd.](https://cutetarantula.com) is a UK based software consultancy that designs, builds and scales modern software systems. Our core expertise spans Blockchain, AI, Fintech, eCommerce and AdTech, but we partner with startups and enterprises across various industries to deliver tailored solutions that meet their unique needs.

As the creator and active participant of the [MysteryForge](https://github.com/MysteryForge) open source lab, our mission is to empower developers, organizations, and enthusiasts through practical innovation and collaboration.
